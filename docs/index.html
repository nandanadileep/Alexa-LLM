Features

◎

Actually answers you

Powered by Gemini, Groq, or any LLM you choose. Not canned responses — real, reasoned answers to whatever you ask.

↺

Remembers the conversation

Context carries through the session. Say yes or no, ask a follow-up — it knows exactly what you're referring to.

◈

Knows who you are

Tell it once — your job, preferences, goals. It stores that and uses it only when relevant, not constantly.

⇄

Swap any model

Switch between Gemini, Groq, or OpenRouter with one environment variable. No code changes ever needed.

◻

Built for voice

Responses are tuned to be spoken aloud — concise, complete, no markdown or bullet points read out awkwardly.

⊕

Costs nothing

AWS Lambda free tier covers typical personal use. All supported LLM providers have free tiers. Roughly $0/month.

Setup guide

01

Enable the skill

Open the Alexa app → Skills & Games → search my assistant → Enable. That's the only step in the app.

02

Say hello

Say Alexa, open my assistant to your device. It'll greet you and ask what you'd like to be called — just answer naturally.

03

Ask anything

Start talking. Say Alexa, ask my assistant followed by whatever's on your mind — questions, ideas, things to think through.

04

Give it your context (optional)

Tell it something once — like remember that I'm a nurse who prefers plain language — and it'll use that when it's actually helpful.

05

Reset anytime

Say forget my context or clear my profile to wipe anything stored and start completely fresh.
