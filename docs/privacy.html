Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

Kimi stores your conversation history and any personal context you choose to share (for example, things you tell the assistant to remember about you). This is stored in a secure database and is associated with your Alexa user ID.

How we use it

Your conversation history is used solely to provide context to the AI so it can give you better, more relevant responses. Your personal context is only used when it is directly relevant to what you are asking. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond making the assistant work for you.

Third-party services

Kimi uses third-party AI providers (such as Google Gemini or Groq) to generate responses. Your queries are sent to these services to produce answers. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for how they handle data.

Data retention

Your conversation history and context are stored until you choose to delete them. You can clear your personal context at any time by saying "forget my context" or "clear my profile" to the assistant.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the service (AI providers listed above).

Children

Kimi is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
